# Expanded Roles and Personas

In this section, we elaborate on the various roles and personas involved in our project. Each role plays a crucial part in the workflow, ensuring collaboration and productivity.

## Roles

1. **Project Manager**: Oversees project timelines and deliverables, facilitating communication between teams.
2. **Developer**: Responsible for coding and implementing features as per the project specifications.
3. **Designer**: Focuses on the visual aspects and user experience of the project.
4. **Tester**: Ensures the product meets quality standards through rigorous testing procedures.

## Personas

- **End User**: The primary user of the product who provides feedback and suggests improvements.
- **Stakeholder**: Individuals or groups with an interest in the project who influence decisions and direction.
- **Administrator**: Manages system settings, user permissions, and troubleshooting issues to ensure smooth operation.
