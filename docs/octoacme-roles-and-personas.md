### New Roles

1. **Change Manager**  
   - **Responsibilities**: Oversee and manage changes across the organization, ensuring minimal disruption to services.  
   - **Goals**: Facilitate smooth transitions during changes; reduce resistance to change.  
   - **Communication Patterns**: Regular updates to stakeholders; coordinate discussions during planning phases.  
   - **Interaction Points**: Works closely with project managers and operations teams.

2. **QA Lead**  
   - **Responsibilities**: Lead quality assurance initiatives, ensuring that products meet quality standards before release.  
   - **Goals**: Improve product quality; reduce defects in production.  
   - **Communication Patterns**: Works closely with development teams; provides feedback during all stages of development.  
   - **Interaction Points**: Collaborates with developers and project stakeholders.

3. **DevOps Facilitator**  
   - **Responsibilities**: Coordinate between development and operations teams to enhance collaboration and streamline processes.  
   - **Goals**: Foster a culture of collaboration; reduce deployment times.  
   - **Communication Patterns**: Acts as a liaison between teams; regular meetings to align goals and resolve conflicts.  
   - **Interaction Points**: Works closely with DevOps engineers and Agile coaches.

4. **Community Liaison**  
   - **Responsibilities**: Serve as the main point of contact between the organization and the community it serves, gathering feedback and facilitating communication.  
   - **Goals**: Improve community engagement; ensure that community needs are met.  
   - **Communication Patterns**: Regular interactions with community members; public meetings; feedback sessions.  
   - **Interaction Points**: Collaborates with marketing and customer support teams.

5. **Process Auditor**  
   - **Responsibilities**: Review and assess the effectiveness of organizational processes; recommend improvements.  
   - **Goals**: Enhance efficiency; ensure compliance with standards and regulations.  
   - **Communication Patterns**: Regular reporting to management; discussions with process owners regarding findings.  
   - **Interaction Points**: Works with compliance teams and project managers.
